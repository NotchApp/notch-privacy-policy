# Notch Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 22, 2026**

## Introduction

Notch ("the app") is a micro-savings habit tracker built for working-class South African adults. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

## Information We Collect

- **Name and email address** — collected during registration to create and secure your account.
- **PIN** — used to authenticate your account. Your PIN is never stored in plain text.
- **Daily commitment amount and saving preferences** — used to personalise your experience.
- **Daily log entries** — records of whether you honoured your daily commitment.
- **Reminder time preference** — used to schedule your daily notification.

## How We Use Your Information

- To create and manage your account
- To track your savings habit progress
- To send you a daily reminder notification at your chosen time
- To display your progress and history within the app

## Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider. All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS.

## Microphone and Camera

The app does not use your microphone or camera. Any permissions requested are inherited from the underlying framework and are not actively used by Notch.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

## Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at notchkeepgoing@gmail.com.

## Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at notchkeepgoing@gmail.com.
